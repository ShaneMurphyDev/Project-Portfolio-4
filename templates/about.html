Our Goal

Welcome to the one and only Zoo and conservation in kerry. Located in the beautiful town of Tralee with ample parking and amenities located on sight, our animals have been the wonder and delight to all visitors since 1969. Jump into the wild and meet our vast range of animals from across the globe, from gorillas to Cheetah, and elephants to meerkats. Learn about how these creatures live in the wild and our conservation efforts to help save endangered species. Whether it's a family day out, a school class, or a group of friends we offer something for everyone.
